  # Pre-PI Planning (Three Weeks Prior)

#Purpose
- Align vision, confirm PI Objectives, prepare program/backlog, map dependencies, and set readiness.

#Cadence & readiness
- Cadence: three weeks before PI
- Outcomes: draft PI plan, dependency map, and ready backlog

Key activities
- Stakeholder alignment and vision grounding
- Backlog readiness and DoR alignment
- Cross-team dependency mapping
- Capacity and readiness checks
- Tool harmonization & visibility (state checks in Jira Align, Jira Cloud, Azure DevOps, ServiceNow, Vision Tools)

Artifacts
- Vision-to-PI traceability notes
- DoR references per feature
- Dependency log and risk log
- Draft Program Board

Deliverables (by end of Pre-PI)
- Confirmed PI Objectives
- Up-to-date program backlog with DoR
- Mapped dependencies and risk mitigations
# Stakeholder Alignment & Vision Grounding

Purpose
- Align stakeholders on the PI vision, objectives, and non-negotiables.

What I do
- Facilitate a short alignment session with Product Management, System/Solution Architects, and key stakeholders
- Capture decisions, open questions, and a concise recap

Artifacts
- Alignment notes
- List of open questions
- Updated PI Objectives reference
# Backlog Readiness & DoR Alignment

Purpose
- Ensure top-level Epics/Features are well-defined with Definition of Ready (DoR) criteria.

What I do
- Verify DoR criteria exist for each high-priority item
- Ensure teams have visibility into upcoming PI scope
- Prepare DoR checklist per feature

Artifacts
- DoR references per feature
- Updated backlog items with criteria
# Cross-Team Dependency Mapping

Purpose
- Identify dependencies and architectural enablers across teams early.

What I do
- Create initial dependency log
- Flag critical blockers and escalation paths

Artifacts
- Dependency Log
- Cross-team risk notes
# Capacity & Readiness Planning

Purpose
- Align team capacity with PI scope and rough estimates.

What I do
- Gather capacity signals
- Validate readiness of planning inputs (draft plans, acceptance criteria)

Artifacts
- Capacity assumptions document
- Early rough estimates
# Tool Harmonization & Visibility (Pre-PI)

Purpose
- Ensure Jira Align, Jira Cloud, Azure DevOps, ServiceNow, Vision Tools reflect latest planning state.

What I do
- Sync dashboards and program boards
- Prepare visibility for Day 1 briefing

Artifacts
- Program boards (draft)
- Dashboards links
# During PI Planning (Day 1 & Day 2)

Purpose
- Execute the committed PI plan with cross-team coordination and clear ownership.

Cadence & timeboxing
- Day 1: Context, goals, architecture, team breakouts
- Day 2: Plan review, confidence vote, finalization

Key activities
- Facilitation, guardrails, and timeboxing
- Synthesis, alignment, decision making
- Coaching and team engagement (remote-friendly)
- Risk and impediment management
- Program Board maintenance and dependency resolution
- Confidence voting and commitment
- Post-event actions

Artifacts
- Updated PI Objectives
- Program Board with dependencies and milestones
- Risk mitigations and owners
- Iteration 1 alignment notes
- DoR/DoD alignment snapshots
# Day 1 – PI Planning

Agenda (typical)
1) Context & Vision refresh (15–30 min)
2) PI Goals & Features preview (60–90 min)
3) Architecture & Enablers (60–90 min)
4) Team Breakouts: Draft Plan (90–120 min)
5) Draft Program Board & Dependencies (60–90 min)

Outcomes
- Draft team plans
- Initial program board dependencies
- Early risk flags and mitigations
# Day 2 – PI Planning

Agenda (typical)
1) Plan Review & Confidence Vote (60–90 min)
2) Finalize PI Objectives & Risks (60–90 min)
3) Risks & Impediments Discussion (30–60 min)
4) Roadmap to Execution (60–90 min)

Outcomes
- Final PI Objectives
- Committed PI plan and risk mitigations
- Iteration 1 alignment notes
